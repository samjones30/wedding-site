The venue is at Northside Farm, Horlsey, Northumberland; about 25minutes west of Newcastle.


We would love you to stay on site, if you fancy. There is some on site accommodation (at a small price!) if you want to stay. See below for more details.

We have the venue from Tuesday 20th till Thursday 22nd August. The ceremony itself is Wednesday 21st August 2019


We plan on having a pre-nuptials party at the venue on the Tuesday night for all guests, and “an actual” breakfast at the venue on the Thursday.

Accomodation

You have a few options:

Stay on site? Sure, we have wig-wams (wooden huts) and tepees (posh tents with a proper bed) available for between £100 & £150. Let us know if you want one in the RSVP form and we'll get you sorted out. The newly-weds will be staying onsite both nights, so come join the party!


Glamping sound grim to you? It's not for everyone. We're about 20 minutes from Newcastle Airport, 25 minutes from Newcastle Central and 40 from central Durham. We can help you source some local taxis if needs be


There are also some nice hotels and Inns in the local area, including Matfen Hall.


Whatever you choose, please let us know in the RSVP.
